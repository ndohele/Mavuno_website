About us

Farmers first. Water that works. Support that stays.

Mavuno is a Tanzanian startup built with communities — not for them — combining practical water infrastructure with hands-on farming education.

Mission

To make smallholder farming in rural Tanzania climate-resilient, productive, and financially sustainable — by delivering affordable water infrastructure and farming knowledge directly to the farmers who need it most.

Our story

Mavuno Water Solutions was founded to solve a problem we saw up close: smallholder farmers in Kishinda Village, Sengerema District, Mwanza Region losing harvests every dry season because they had no reliable water access — and no way to easily reach the services that could help them. We built a model that goes to the farmer, not the other way around.

Currently operating in: Kishinda Village, Sengerema District, Mwanza Region, Tanzania
