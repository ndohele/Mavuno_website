Our solutions

Four integrated services — all delivered to your village

Everything we do is designed for smallholder realities: seasonal income, shared equipment, and learning that happens in the field — not in a classroom far away.

Irrigation systems

Simple river and lake intake systems, and wells where no surface water exists. Clean, reliable water for crops year-round — regardless of rainfall.

Cost: TZS 3,000,000–5,000,000 depending on farm size and distance from water source.

Drainage systems

Flood protection channels that redirect excess rainwater safely away from crops, preventing soil erosion and harvest loss during heavy rains.

Technical support

We don’t walk away after installation. Our field team conducts regular maintenance visits and rapid repairs, keeping systems operational season after season.

Farming education

Hands-on training in irrigation operation, land management, fertiliser use, and pest control — building skills that give farmers lasting independence.
